88RISING's Mission

Sean Miyashiro has always been clear about the mission statement for his company, 88rising: to support and promote Asian artists, creatives and communities through its label, management and marketing wings. And during Asian American and Pacific Islander (AAPI) Heritage Month, Miyashiro and 88rising have doubled down on that commitment with a series of programming events and discussions in a variety of ways, including on its own SiriusXM channel, 88rising Radio.

“I get to represent where I’m from and bring my culture on these songs.” -Sean Miyashiro

Artist of the Month

JOJI

George Kusunoki Miller (ジョージ・楠木・ミラー, Jōji Kusunoki Mirā, born 18/09/1992), known professionally as Joji, is a Japanese singer, rapper, comedian, and former YouTuber. Miller's music has been described as a mix between RnB, lo-fi and trip hop.

PINK SEASON : THE PROPHECY

Pink Season: The Prophecy is an extended play by YouTube personality George "Joji" Miller, under the alias Pink Guy. It was released on 24 May 2017 under the 88rising label and features remixes of songs from the album Pink Season .This would be the final release done by George Miller under the Pink Guy alias.
